## Privacy Policy

**intro-skipper · Last updated: April 1, 2026**

---

## Overview

This document describes how the Segment Editor mobile application collects, handles, and processes data. Our approach is privacy-first: we minimise data collection, store credentials only on your device in encrypted form, and are transparent about every third party involved.

---

## App Privacy Policy

Applies to: **Segment Editor** — published on Google Play.

### Data Collection

The app does not collect any personal information beyond what is strictly required to connect to your Jellyfin server:

- **Server URL** — the address of your Jellyfin server instance.
- **Username and password** — used solely to authenticate with your Jellyfin server.
- **API key** — obtained from your Jellyfin server upon successful login and used for subsequent requests.

No analytics, crash-reporting SDKs, advertising identifiers, or any other tracking mechanisms are present in the app.

### Data Storage

All credentials (server URL, username, password, and API key) are stored locally on your device using Android's encrypted storage. No credential or preference data is transmitted to intro-skipper or any third party.

### Data Transmitted

The app communicates exclusively with the Jellyfin server address that you provide. No data is sent to intro-skipper servers or any third-party service operated by intro-skipper.

Content displayed beyond standard settings and dialogs (such as images) is fetched directly from your Jellyfin server and is not retained beyond standard Android image-caching mechanisms.

### Data Controller

intro-skipper is the data controller for any data processed by this app. Contact us at [info@intro-skipper.org](mailto:info@intro-skipper.org).

### Your Rights

Even though the app does not collect personal data beyond locally stored credentials, you retain all rights granted by applicable privacy law (including GDPR where applicable). To exercise any right, please contact us at the address above or open an issue in the project repository.

You may delete all locally stored data at any time by clearing the app's data via Android Settings or by uninstalling the app.

## Third-Party Services

### Google Play

The app is distributed through Google Play. Google may collect device and usage information subject to Google's own privacy policy: [https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

### Your Jellyfin Server

All MediaSegment API data is read from and written to the Jellyfin server you configure. That server is operated by you or a third party of your choosing and is subject to their own privacy practices.
